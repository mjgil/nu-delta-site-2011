Summer Housing

Prospective Summer Boarder, If you’re staying in Boston for the summer and looking for affordable place to live, it’s time to consider spending your summer at Nu Delta (ΝΔ). While living at Nu Delta, you will find yourself in the middle of one of the top social scenes and living environments, complemented with all of the modern amenities and, of course, the convenience of living in Boston.

Our Housing

Living at Nu Delta is unlike living at any other fraternity. Take a look at the virtual house tour to get a glimpse of what the house is like. Our historical house is located in the beautiful Boston Back Bay at 460 Beacon Street close to Beacon and Mass Ave. MIT is less than ten minutes away across the Harvard Bridge and Kenmore and the Hynes Convention Center T Stops are both located within a five minute walk away. We are also close to Fenway Park, Copley Square, and the Prudential Center.

Amenities

Our house has many amenities that make living here not just comfortable but also relaxing. We have a library, plasma TV with cable and OnDemand, Nintendo Wii, VCR, and DVD player. Feeling hungry? You’ll be free to cook in our huge and fully-equipped kitchen. What about laundry? We have a dryer and have just replaced our old washer with a new state of the art washing machine, both available free of charge. All rooms have high-speed Internet connections and Wi-Fi is available throughout the house. Our rooms come fully furnished including beds, mattresses, closet space, desks, and couches.

Housing Info

Doubles, triples, and quads are available to rent. Move-in date is Sunday, May 23rd, 2011 and Move-Out date is Saturday, August 14th, 2011. Residents will be allowed full use of all the house facilities over the summer. Guests are allowed, but at the discretion of the summer house managers. We will be having a 4th of July party and all residents invited. All other social events are coordinated with the summer house managers.

Pricing

Pricing for summer housing for summer 2012 will be available starting February 2012. Generally prices are between the $1400 to $1800 ranges for the summer.

Parking

Parking spaces in our back lot can be rented for $400 for the summer.
